# GTA: City Stories

A browser-based GTA-style game built with HTML5, CSS3, and vanilla JavaScript.

## Features

- **CJ-Style Character**: Playable protagonist with full movement controls
- **Weapon System**: Multiple weapons including fists, pistols, SMGs, rifles, and shotguns
- **Combat Mechanics**: Aim with mouse, shoot, reload, and manage ammunition
- **Vehicle System**: Drive cars around the city
- **Mission System**: 4 story-driven missions with objectives
- **Shop System**: Buy weapons, ammo, health, and armor
- **GTA-Style HUD**: Health, armor, wanted level, minimap, and weapon display
- **AI NPCs**: Non-hostile NPCs walking around the city
- **Enemies**: AI enemies that chase and shoot the player
- **Dynamic City**: Procedurally generated buildings and streets
- **Minimap**: Real-time radar showing player and entities

## Controls

| Key | Action |
|-----|--------|
| **W/A/S/D** | Move character |
| **Mouse** | Aim weapon |
| **Left Click** | Fire weapon |
| **1-5** | Switch weapons |
| **E** | Interact/Buy items |
| **F** | Enter/Exit vehicle |
| **R** | Reload weapon |
| **H** | Horn (in vehicle) |
| **M** | Mission select |
| **ESC** | Pause game |

## Game Features

### Missions
1. **Tutorial Mission** - Learn combat basics by defeating 5 enemies
2. **Destroy the Rival Gang** - Eliminate 10 enemy gang members
3. **Steal the Vehicle** - Drive to a destination
4. **Protect the VIP** - Defend against attackers for 60 seconds

### Weapons
- **Fists** - Melee combat, unlimited ammo
- **9mm Pistol** - Standard firearm
- **SMG** - Fast firing weapon
- **Rifle** - Long range, high damage
- **Shotgun** - Close range, massive damage

### Shop Items
- Health Pack (+50 health)
- Armor (+50 armor)
- Ammunition for various weapons

## Game Mechanics

- **Health & Armor**: Protect yourself from damage
- **Wanted Level**: Increase wanted level by committing crimes
- **Money System**: Earn money from missions and spend it at shops
- **AI Enemies**: Fight against enemy AI that shoots back
- **NPC Crowds**: Pedestrians walking around the city
- **Vehicle Driving**: Enter any vehicle and drive around

## How to Play

1. Open the game in your browser
2. Click "Start Game" from the main menu
3. Use WASD to move around the city
4. Use mouse to aim and left-click to shoot
5. Collect weapons from shops by pressing E near NPCs
6. Complete missions from the mission menu (Press M)
7. Increase your wanted level and evade police
8. Explore the procedurally generated city

## Technical Details

- **Engine**: Canvas-based 2D game engine
- **Physics**: Basic velocity and gravity system
- **Rendering**: Optimized canvas rendering with camera follow
- **AI**: Simple state-based AI for NPCs and enemies
- **Performance**: 60 FPS target with frame rate counter

## Installation

1. Clone this repository
2. Open `index.html` in a modern web browser
3. Enjoy the game!
